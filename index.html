F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Docker?

    Docker has grown in popularity to the point where it has become synonymous with container technology. Docker established the industry standard for containers and solved the "it works on my machine" problem for millions of developers around the world. It allows developers to simply package, deploy, and execute any programme as a lightweight, self-contained container that can run almost anywhere.

  • What is Container?

    Containers are a solution to one of the most important conventional problems in the DevOps software development process: "how to get software to operate consistently when moved from one computing environment to another." It enables us to develop, test, deploy, and redeploy apps across a variety of settings, including local machines, on-premises data centres, and private and public clouds.

  • Why should you learn Docker?

    Docker is a programme that allows developers to create, manage, and distribute containers locally. Docker is one of the most in-demand skills among the DevOps toolchain, with salaries exceeding $100,000 in the United States. Docker is used by top organizations such as Adobe, SAP, VMware, and IBM in their DevOps techniques.

  • Why Docker is so useful?

    In the DevOps pipeline, Docker is one of the most significant and extensively used tools. Containers simplify the process of developing, deploying, and executing applications.

  • What types of jobs are available for Docker Certified Associates?

    You can pursue a career in DevOps after learning Docker. Docker is a critical component of the DevOps process. Individuals with Docker certification are qualified for the following jobs:

    • DevOps engineer
    • Release manager
    • DevOps consultant
    • DevOps architect

  • What kind of learning materials will I receive after the training?

    After the course, each participant will receive LIFETIME ACCESS to our Learning Management System (LMS), which includes materials such as class recordings, notes, PDF slides, Web reference step-by-step guide, questions and answers, Dumps, test module, exercise, and assignements.

  • What steps do I need to take to become certified?

    To achieve a Docker Certification, you must complete a proctored exam with a passing score. You will receive your certification credentials once you have received a passing score.

  • How can I sign up for the Docker Certified Associate exam?

    This exam is proctored by Examity, and you must register and schedule the exam through their website. DCA - Exam - Registration

  • What is the duration of my official Docker certification?

    Docker (DCA) Certification is only good for two years. Following that, you must renew your certification every two years.

  • Can I retake the exam if I don't pass the first time?

    If you don't pass a Docker Certification exam the first time, you can retake it under the following conditions: a) wait 14 days after failing an exam before attempting it again; b) pay the exam fee each time you attempt an exam.

  • How much does the Docker (DCA) certification exam cost?

    Yes, the Associate Exam registration price is $195 or €175. One exam attempt is included in the payment.
